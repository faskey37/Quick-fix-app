About John Electrics

Hello! I'm John, a licensed electrician with over 8 years of experience serving the New York area. I take pride in delivering high-quality electrical services with a focus on safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

My journey in the electrical field began with a 4-year apprenticeship program, followed by obtaining my master electrician license. I've worked on everything from small residential repairs to large commercial installations.

When I'm not helping customers with their electrical needs, you can find me teaching electrical safety workshops at the local community center or spending time with my family.
